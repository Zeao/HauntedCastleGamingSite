Origins

Origins is a fantasy based trading card-board game. It combines the intrigue and individuality of a regular trading card game, along with the strategy and spacial-reasoning of a board game.

In Origins, you take on the persona of a powerful champion and battle you opponents to the death in the confines of a coliseum arena. You need to master the manipulation of Aura, and use your Energy to cast powerful spells, beckon epic summons and perform amazing techniques.

As you trade cards with your friends, you can make more powerful decks and prove your own strengths
