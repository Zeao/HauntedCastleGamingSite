About Haunted Castle Gaming.

Haunted Castle Gaming is an individually owned gaming company in Toronto. We are currently working on our first product: Origins.

Part of our mission at Haunted Castle Gaming is to empower the players of our games to suggest ideas and feel their ideas influence the games they play. Please feel free to e-mail us at any time at OriginsBCG@gmail.com, submit card ideas bellow or fill out our survey bellow to give us your opinion on your experience.
